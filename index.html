About the Campaign

Green Earth is a global reforestation initiative dedicated to fighting climate change. Our goal is to plant over 500,000 trees worldwide by creating carbon sinks, restoring habitats, and combating global warming.

  • Restoration of ecosystems
  • Improvement of local air quality
  • Support for local communities
